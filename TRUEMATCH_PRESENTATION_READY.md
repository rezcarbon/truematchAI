**"What about the patent moat?"**
→ *"The governance framework IS the moat. We're compliant with EU AI Act Art. 12-15 by default. Competitors have to bolt compliance on later. We built it first."*

**"How do you compare to traditional ATS?"**
→ *"They assess keywords. We assess capability. Traditional ATS can give you a high-scoring candidate who can't actually do the job. We find the hidden gems — people with demonstrated ability who just don't use the right buzzwords."*

**"Why should I trust your AI?"**
→ *"Every assessment is auditable. You can see what Claude analyzed, why it scored the way it did, and why our gates flagged it (if they did). No black box. Plus, it's Claude Opus — the best model available."*
