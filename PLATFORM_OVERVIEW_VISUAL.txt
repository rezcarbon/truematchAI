🎯 PLATFORM AT A GLANCE

WHAT IS TRUEMATCH?
  Capability-First ATS powered by Claude AI
  Evaluates: What candidates can actually DO (not just keywords)

CORE INSIGHT:
  Traditional ATS: Keyword matching (too simplistic, misses talent)
  TrueMatch: 6-pillar assessment + governance gates + audit trail

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR:
  Counter-recommendations when capability >> keyword matching

🧠 6-PILLAR ASSESSMENT SYSTEM

Every candidate is evaluated on 6 independent dimensions:

Pillar 1: TRADITIONAL ATS (Keyword Baseline)
  What: Traditional keyword matching
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Establishes baseline (might be wrong)

Pillar 2: SEMANTIC MATCHING (Concept Matching)
  What: Concept-level matching beyond keywords
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Catches nuance keyword matching misses

Pillar 3: CAPABILITY ASSESSMENT (Can they do it?)
  What: What can candidate actually do?
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Answers the real question

Pillar 4: TRAJECTORY ANALYSIS (Career growth)
  What: Career progression, stability, growth
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Predicts success factors

Pillar 5: JD INTERROGATION (Is role clear?)
  What: Is the job description realistic?
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Bad JDs produce bad hires

Pillar 6: EVIDENCE VERIFICATION (Can we verify?)
  What: LinkedIn, GitHub, references
  Score: 0-100
  Why: Trust but verify

COMPOSITE SCORE = Average of all 6 pillars
COUNTER-REC = When Capability >> Traditional (delta > 20)
